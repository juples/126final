About Us

Wendy Ortega Founded Faith Hope Love Cleaning in 2014. She is a mother of 4 wonderful children and is based in High Point, North Carolina. Wendy was born in Queens, New York but moved to High Point very early in her life, she grew up immersed in Salvadoran and American culture. Wendy regularly attends church and volunteers as a Sunday School teacher. She began her career as a customer service representative before founding Faith Hope Love Cleaning. Wendy has always had a passion for providing excellent customer service and housekeeping and always strives to give her clients the best experience. As a mother herself, Wendy understands the stress that comes cleaning a house with children.
